§ 04
Revisions
This agreement includes 2 rounds of revisions. A revision round is defined as one consolidated list of feedback submitted in a single message or document.

Additional revision rounds beyond 2 are billed at $75 per round, invoiced before implementation. Revisions must be submitted within 5 business days of the staging link being shared. Revisions not submitted within this window are considered accepted.
§ 05
Ownership & Rights
Upon receipt of full and final payment, the client owns full rights to the final deliverables including all code, design files, and assets created specifically for this project.

Xenotrix retains the right to: display the completed work in its portfolio, case studies, and marketing materials, unless the client requests otherwise in writing.
§ 06
Client Responsibilities
  • Provide all required content (text, images, logo, brand details) within 48 hours of project kickoff.
  • Be available for a 15-minute review call on Day 4 (staging review) and Day 7 (launch).
  • Provide consolidated feedback — not piecemeal messages spread over several days.
  • Ensure all content submitted is owned by the client or licensed for use. Xenotrix is not liable for third-party copyright issues arising from client-provided materials.
  • Make payments on the agreed schedule. Work pauses if payment is more than 3 days overdue.
§ 07
Cancellation
The client may cancel this project at any time in writing. Cancellation terms:

• Before work begins: Full deposit refunded minus a $50 admin fee.
• After work begins (before staging): 50% deposit is non-refundable.
• After staging is delivered: Full deposit is non-refundable. No final payment required if client cancels before launch.

Xenotrix may cancel the agreement if the client fails to provide required content within 14 days of kickoff or fails to make payments. In this case, all work completed to date is owned by Xenotrix until full payment is received.
