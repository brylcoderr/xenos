01 ‚Äî CORE STRATEGY

Phone-First Outreach System

Since you have phone numbers ‚Äî not emails ‚Äî your primary channels are WhatsApp Business, cold calling, and SMS. Here's how to work each.

Channel 1: WhatsApp Business

Highest open rate of any channel. 90%+ reads within 3 minutes.

PRIMARY
  • Set up WhatsApp Business account with Xenotrix branding, xenotrix.com link, and service catalog
  • Use WhatsApp Web to batch-send messages ‚Äî 50‚Äì80 contacts per day manually (free)
  • Set auto-reply: "Thanks for replying! Book a free call: xenotrix.com/book"
  • Use WATI free trial (3 days) or WA-Automate self-hosted for automation
  • Create message templates: Website Audit, Free Landing Page Offer, Speed Fix
  • Follow up every non-reply after 48 hours with a different message angle

Channel 2: Cold Calling

Fastest path to a decision. One call can close a deal same day.

HIGH IMPACT
  • Call 20‚Äì30 prospects per day minimum. Takes about 2 hours total.
  • Target: local B2B businesses ‚Äî restaurants, clinics, lawyers, real estate, gyms
  • Use the "10-second hook" script: state problem you solve before pitching anything
  • Goal of call: NOT to sell. Goal is to book a 15-min Zoom/Meet call
  • Track every call in your CRM dashboard: Answered / Voicemail / Interested / Rejected
  • Callback rate is 15‚Äì20% ‚Äî follow up every lead that didn't answer

Channel 3: SMS Outreach

Short, direct, no fluff. Works when WhatsApp doesn't deliver.

SECONDARY
  • Use TextBelt (free tier: 1/day) or Textbee (open-source self-hosted) for batch SMS
  • Keep SMS under 160 characters. One clear CTA only.
  • Template: "Hi [Name], I help [business type] get more clients with fast websites. Free audit? Reply YES. ‚Äî Xenotrix"
  • Send SMS only to leads that didn't respond to WhatsApp after 72 hours

Channel 4: Find Their Emails

Phone numbers ‚Üí find emails for multi-channel attack.

ENRICHMENT
  • Use Hunter.io free (25/month): paste company domain ‚Üí get email
  • Use Apollo.io free (50 exports/month): search company ‚Üí find contact emails
  • Use LinkedIn: search company name ‚Üí message on LinkedIn for free (50/week)
  • Manual lookup: visit their website ‚Üí look for Contact/Team page
  • Enrich 10‚Äì20 top prospects per day to build an email list alongside phone list

06 ‚Äî SALES FUNNEL

The 7-Step Conversion Pipeline

STEP 1
Lead Identification
Segment your 2500 leads by business type. Prioritize: restaurants, clinics, lawyers, gyms, real estate agents. These have budget and visible website pain.
STEP 2
Research (2 min per lead)
Visit their website. Note 1‚Äì2 specific problems: slow load, broken mobile, no contact button, no Google Business, etc. Personalization wins replies.
STEP 3
WhatsApp Outreach
Send Template 1 with their specific problem mentioned. If no reply in 48 hours, send follow-up. If no reply after that, try cold call. Log every action in CRM.
STEP 4
Discovery Call (15 min)
Ask the 7 discovery questions. Do NOT pitch yet. Listen. Your goal: understand their pain deeply so your proposal is impossible to say no to.
STEP 5
Proposal (sent within 2 hours)
Use call notes + Claude AI to generate custom proposal. Send as PDF. Valid 7 days. Follow up if no response within 48 hours.
STEP 6
Invoice + 50% Deposit
Use Wave (free) or Stripe (free) to send invoice. Work begins only after deposit. This confirms commitment and funds your delivery costs.
STEP 7
Delivery + Retainer Pitch
Deliver in 7 days or less. On launch day, pitch the maintenance retainer. "We can keep this running for $200/month ‚Äî want us to handle it so you never worry about the site?"
