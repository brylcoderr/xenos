02 ‚Äî Main Script
The Full Cold Call
Read this out loud 10 times before your first call. Own every word.
Phase 1 ¬∑ Opening
The 30-Second Opener
MEMORIZE THIS
YOU "Hi, is this [Name]?"
THEM Yes / Who is this?
YOU "Great ‚Äî I will be super quick, I promise. My name is [Your Name] from Xenotrix. We help [business type ‚Äî restaurant / clinic / gym] owners get more customers from their website."
NOTE Pause 1 second here. Let them absorb. Do NOT keep talking immediately.
YOU "I just checked [Company]'s website ‚Äî I noticed one specific thing that is probably costing you inquiries every week. I am not selling anything right now. I just want to show you what I found on a 10-minute call."
YOU "Are you free Wednesday or Thursday this week ‚Äî even 10 minutes?"
Critical Rule

Your goal is NOT to sell on this call. Your only goal is to book a 15-minute discovery call. The moment you start pitching services, you lose. Stay focused on "I want to show you what I found."

Phase 2 ¬∑ If They Say YES
Booking the Discovery Call
BOOK FAST
THEM "Sure, what's this about?"
YOU "Perfect. I found that [specific issue ‚Äî e.g., your contact form does not work on mobile / your site takes 9 seconds to load / there is no WhatsApp button]. A lot of potential customers are leaving without contacting you because of this."
YOU "I can walk you through the fix and what it would look like for [Company] specifically. Takes 15 minutes on a Google Meet. I am sending you a booking link on WhatsApp right now ‚Äî what is the best number?"
NOTE Send the Cal.com link on WhatsApp WHILE on the call. Confirm they received it before hanging up.

The Soft Exit (Keep the Door Open)
NEVER BURN
THEM "I am busy right now / not interested / we already have someone."
YOU "Totally understand ‚Äî I appreciate you picking up. No pitch, I promise. Can I just send you a quick WhatsApp message with what I found? Takes 2 seconds to read, and if it is useful, great ‚Äî if not, no problem at all."
NOTE 90% will say yes. Send WhatsApp Template 1 within 5 minutes. This call is not lost ‚Äî it is warming up.

04 ‚Äî Prep
Before Every Call ‚Äî 2-Minute Prep
Never call cold blind. 2 minutes of research = 3x higher conversion.
  • Visit their website. Note the load speed (use PageSpeed Insights ‚Äî free).
  • Check if the contact form works on mobile. Try submitting it.
  • Check if they have a Google Business Profile. Is it complete?
  • Check if there is a WhatsApp or click-to-call button on the homepage.
  • Look at their last Google review date. Old reviews = disengaged from online presence.
  • Write down 1 specific issue to mention. This is your hook ‚Äî memorize it for the call.
  • Have your Cal.com link ready to paste on WhatsApp immediately after the call.
The Specificity Rule

"I noticed your website has some issues" = ignored. "I noticed your site takes 11 seconds to load on mobile and Google penalizes anything over 3 seconds" = they listen. One specific data point beats any pitch deck.

05 ‚Äî After
Post-Call Actions (Do These Immediately)

  • Interested but no time: Send Loom audit video on WhatsApp within 5 minutes. Set follow-up date to tomorrow.
  • Voicemail: Send WhatsApp Template 1 immediately. Set follow-up date to +48 hours. Log "VM Left."
  • Not interested: Log objection type in Notes. Send nothing today. Schedule a WhatsApp in 30 days.
  • Wrong number / no answer: Log and move to next lead. Do not waste time. Volume is the game.
